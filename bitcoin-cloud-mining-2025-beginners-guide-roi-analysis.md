## What is Bitcoin Cloud Mining?

Bitcoin cloud mining is a service that allows you to rent mining power from remote data centers instead of buying and maintaining your own hardware. Think of it like renting a powerful computer that mines Bitcoin 24/7â€”you pay for the computing power, and the mining company handles all the technical details.

### How Cloud Mining Actually Works

When you purchase a cloud mining contract, you're essentially renting a specific amount of "hash power"â€”the computational power used to solve complex mathematical problems that secure the Bitcoin network. The mining provider operates massive data centers filled with specialized ASIC miners, and your rented hash power contributes to their overall mining operations.

Here's the basic process:
1. You choose a contract specifying hash power amount and duration
2. The provider allocates that mining power to you
3. Their equipment mines Bitcoin on your behalf
4. You receive daily payouts based on your share of the mining rewards
